# Thoughts In a Box

> *Organizing the Mental Clutter*

A compassionate mental health platform designed to help individuals organize their thoughts, identify stress triggers, and discover practical steps toward mental wellness through guided questionnaires and personalized insights.

## About

**Thoughts In a Box** is a mental health web application that provides a safe, private, and judgment-free space for individuals to:

- **Reflect** on their thoughts and emotions through guided questionnaires
- **Identify** stress patterns and triggers
- **Discover** personalized coping strategies and resources
- **Access** curated mindfulness tools and professional support

### The Problem We're Solving

Millions of people worldwide struggle with mental health challenges but face barriers such as:
- Feeling overwhelmed by scattered thoughts
- Difficulty identifying what's causing their stress
- Stigma around seeking help
- Limited access to affordable mental health resources

### Our Solution

We provide an accessible, affordable, and private digital platform that meets users where they are, helping them organize their mental clutter and take meaningful steps toward wellness.

## Design Philosophy

### Color Psychology
Our cyan and teal color scheme was carefully chosen based on color psychology:
- **Cyan (#06B6D4)** - Promotes calmness and clarity
- **Teal (#14B8A6)** - Evokes trust and emotional balance
- **Slate Gray** - Provides grounding and stability

### User Experience Principles
1. **Simplicity** - Clean, uncluttered interface reduces cognitive load
2. **Empathy** - Compassionate language and gentle interactions
3. **Privacy** - Clear communication about data protection
4. **Accessibility** - WCAG compliant, keyboard navigation, screen reader support
5. **Progressive Disclosure** - Information revealed gradually to avoid overwhelm
